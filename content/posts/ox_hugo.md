## ox-hugo {#ox-hugo}

ox-hugo is an Org exporter backend that exports Org to Hugo-compatible Markdown (Blackfriday) and also generates the front-matter (in TOML or YAML format).
<https://github.com/kaushalmodi/ox-hugo>
